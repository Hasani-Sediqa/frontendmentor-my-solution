About Us

Welcome to Afghani Cuisine, your guide to traditional Afghan cooking. Known for its unique spices, hearty dishes, and delightful flavors, Afghan cuisine features savory kebabs, aromatic rice, and sweet pastries. Our recipes bring the authentic taste of Afghanistan to your kitchen.

Appetizers

Chicken Soup

Ingredients

  • Oil 4 Tbsp
  • 1 Onion & tomato, 3 Garlic
  • 1 Checken brest
  • 1 Cup carrots , Potatoes & cabbage
  • salt, pepper

Instructions

  1. Heat the oil: In a in a large pot over medium heat then Add the chopped onion and sauté until softened and golden.
  2. Stir : in the minced garlic, cumin, coriander, turmeric, paprika, and cinnamon. Cook for about 1 minute until fragrant.
  3. Pour: in the chicken broth and bring to a simmer and Add the shredded chicken, diced tomatoes, and cooked chickpeas. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Simmer: for about 15-20 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
  5. Enjoy: Serve hot, garnished with fresh cilantro and a squeeze of lemon juice.

Main Courses

Qabeli Palaw

The core ingredients are steamed rice mixed with caramelized carrots and dry raisins as well as marinated lamb meat. Qabeli palaw is commonly garnished with almonds and pistachios.

Bolani

Afghan bakers layer bolani with a generous filling of potatoes, spinach or lentils. Fresh herbs and scallions add bright flavor to the chewy, comforting dish.

Mantu

The mantu is a thin dough that is filled with beef or lamb mixed in onions and spices. The steamed foodstuff then tops it off with an orange sauce made from tomatoes

Ashak

Vegetable. Afghan Ashak dumplings are a popular dish. They do take a lot of time but are worth it. Ashak (or “gandana”) are a dumpling filled with leeks and, typically, covered in a meat sauce.

Desserts

FIRNI

In essence it is an eggless milk custard, where milk and sugar are thickened with cornflour and lightly spiced with ground cardamon and flavoured with a dash of heady rose water and topped with a sprinkling of nuts

Shir Bringe

The main ingredients for Shir Berenj Recipe (Afghan Rice Pudding) are rice, milk , cardamom , and sugar . All the other flavorings are optional.
